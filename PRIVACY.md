# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

## What This Extension Does

New Line Guide displays a helpful guide "new line = shift + enter" above text input areas on ChatGPT and Claude websites.

## Data Collection

**This extension does NOT collect any personal data.**

The only data stored locally on your device:
- Extension enabled/disabled setting
- Debug logging on/off setting

These preferences are stored using Chrome's sync storage.

## What We DON'T Collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No typed content
- No analytics or tracking

## Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with anyone. All settings stay on your device.
